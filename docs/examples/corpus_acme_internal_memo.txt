SUMMARY
This memo records decisions about the “Northwind ingestion pipeline” rollout and the related
customer data retention stance. It is written for product, security, and support teams.

1. PIPELINE OVERVIEW
The Northwind ingestion pipeline batches customer support transcripts weekly. Chunks are
deduplicated by ticket_id before embeddings are computed. The pipeline owner is the Platform
team (slack: #platform-ingest).

2. RETENTION POLICY (AS OF 2026-Q1)
Support transcript text is retained for 18 months in the primary object store. Derived
embeddings and FAISS indices are rebuilt monthly; cold backups are kept for 7 years per legal.

3. KNOWN ISSUES
Ticket NW-8891: duplicate chunks were observed when users pasted the same error log twice.
Mitigation shipped in release v2.3.1 (2026-01-15). Ops should verify chunk_id stability when
re-ingesting historical tickets.

4. CUSTOMER-FACING LANGUAGE
Public docs must not promise “instant” search over transcripts; approved phrasing is
“typically available within one business day after ticket closure.”

5. CONTACT
Questions: ingestion-oncall@acme.example (pager on weekends).
